If you are having issues completing a test or are confused as to how to take a test then follow these instructions. When landing on the tests tab you must first pick a test or quiz of the event that you wish to complete. Once you have chosen a test you will take the first question will be a mix and match question. Match the term with it's corresponding definition in order to get a point. These will be completely randomized and in a random format ranging from multiple choice, fill in the blank, drop down, and true or false.

To answer a multiple choice question you must choose an answer from the ones provided. You are allowed to change your answer while you are still in the same question. You will not be able to change your answer once you have clicked the next button.

Fill in the blank will prompt you to write the answer to the question in the text box below. You may change your answer while you are still in this question.

To answer a drop down question you will navigate to the drop down menu provided in your question and then select an answer from the answers provided.
